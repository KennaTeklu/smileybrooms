Installation Instructions:

3 Drag and drop the downloaded IPA file into iTunes
4 Connect your iOS device to your computer
5 Sync your device with iTunes to install the app

Note: For enterprise distribution, you may need to trust the developer in Settings > General > Profiles & Device Management after installation.
